How I Work

My Design Process

01

Discover & Define

I start by deeply understanding your business, target users, and goals. Stakeholder interviews, competitor analysis, and user research form the foundation.

02

Wireframe & Structure

Low-fidelity wireframes map out the information architecture and user flows before any visual design begins. Structure first, beauty second.

03

Design & Prototype

High-fidelity designs in Figma with interactive prototypes. Every pixel is intentional — typography, spacing, color, and motion all harmonized.

04

Test & Iterate

Real user testing, heatmaps, and feedback loops. I refine until the experience is frictionless and delightful.

05

Handoff & Support

Clean developer-ready Figma files with specs, assets, and design tokens. I stay available through the development cycle.
